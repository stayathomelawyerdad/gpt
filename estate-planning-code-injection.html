Estate Planning Services We Provide

Thoughtfully crafted plans to protect your loved ones, preserve your assets, and honor your wishes at every stage of life.

Core Planning Documents

Lay a solid foundation with the essentials every family needs to safeguard their estate.

Last Will & Testament

Direct who inherits your property, appoint an executor, and name guardians for minor children.

Revocable Living Trust

Keep assets organized during life, avoid probate, and choose trusted successor trustee(s).

Property Power of Attorney

Name a financial decision-maker to manage money, property, and transactions if you cannot.

Health Care Power of Attorney

Empower someone you trust to make medical decisions when you are unable to speak for yourself.

Health & Personal Directives

Document your medical wishes and give loved ones the access they need to support you.

Advance Health Care Directive

Share your preferences for end-of-life care, life support, and pain management.

HIPAA Authorization

Grant trusted individuals the ability to review medical records and speak with providers.

Asset-Specific Tools

Fine-tune your plan for specific property so everything transfers smoothly.

Transfer on Death Deed

Transfer real estate directly to beneficiaries and bypass probate proceedings.

Personal Property Memorandum

Designate who receives heirlooms and collections without altering your Will or Trust.

Beneficiary Designation Review

Align life insurance, retirement plans, and POD/TOD accounts with your estate plan.

Family Protection Tools

Provide care instructions and financial safeguards for the people who depend on you.

Guardian Nomination

Nominate trusted caregivers for minor children if you become incapacitated or pass away.

Special Needs Trust

Support loved ones with disabilities while protecting their eligibility for key benefits.

Pet Trust & Care Plan

Set aside funds and instructions to ensure pets receive lifetime care.

Other Planning Options

Customize your strategy with advanced tools for complex assets and long-term goals.

Irrevocable Trusts

Design trusts for tax efficiency, asset protection, and long-term care planning.

Business Succession Planning

Map out future ownership, management, and leadership transitions for your company.

Legacy Letters

Share personal guidance, stories, and values to accompany your legal documents.
